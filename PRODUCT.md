# DG Projects

## Product Purpose
Full-service digital growth agency helping small and medium businesses establish, optimize, and scale their online presence. Not a freelancer portfolio — a professional agency offering web design, SEO, maintenance, digital presence management, and strategy for businesses that want real results online.

## Users
Primary: Small to medium business owners in El Salvador who need a professional web presence and ongoing digital support. They are non-technical; they think in business outcomes ("will this get me more customers?"), not code. They are evaluating agencies, comparing options, and need to feel trust quickly.

Secondary: Regional businesses expanding online — clinics, logistics companies, retail, restaurants, service businesses.

## Brand Tone
Professional. Direct. Outcome-oriented. Authority without arrogance. We speak the language of business results, not developer features. "Your customers will find you on Google" not "we implement technical SEO." Warm but competent.

## Anti-references
- Personal portfolio / freelancer sites
- Generic template agency clones
- "Award-winning" agencies that brag but don't show results
- Galaxy/cosmic/space aesthetics — too personal, too developer-coded
- Sites that lead with technical skills instead of business outcomes
- Flat agency pages that look like a Webflow template from 2021

## Strategic Principles
1. Every headline communicates a business outcome, not a service feature
2. Every section has a clear next action
3. Services framed as solutions to known business problems
4. Trust signals before pricing
5. Conversion-first hierarchy: awareness → interest → desire → action
6. Show results, not just capabilities
